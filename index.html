Kexin Wang

Postdoctoral Research Fellow in Plant Science | Freelance Interpreter

I'm a scientist with a background in marine microbiology, biochemistry and plant science. Throughout my career, I’ve been passionate about translating research into real-world solutions. I enjoy diving into data, solving complex problems and improving workflows to make processes more efficient and effective.

Collaborating with people from different fields has been a rewarding part of my journey, whether it’s working across departments or partnering with external researchers. I thrive in dynamic environments where challenges evolve quickly, and I’m constantly learning and adapting to new ideas. At the same time, I value the opportunity to think independently and contribute my own insights to the projects I’m involved in.

I’ve also had the privilege of engaging with the public, from sharing scientific knowledge through exhibits to interpreting for diverse audiences. This experience has strengthened my ability to communicate complex concepts clearly and connect with people in meaningful ways.

Looking forward, I’m excited about opportunities in biotech and tech, where I can continue solving problems, collaborating with talented teams, and contributing to impactful projects.
