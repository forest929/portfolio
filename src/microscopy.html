Advanced Microscopy

PhD Research | Queen Mary University of London | 2019–2023

I bring advanced proficiency in a wide range of microscopy platforms, with particular expertise in RNA-FISH for spatial gene expression analysis in cyanobacteria. My experience extends to image quantification with ImageJ and employing advanced 3D imaging techniques, including SIM2 processing via Zeiss Zen Black/Blue. These skills have enabled me to generate precise, high-quality data that drives key insights in my research.

  • Leica STELLARIS 8 Confocal Microscope
  • Super-resolution Zeiss Elyra PS.1
  • Super-resolution Elyra 7 SIM²
